for making schezwan noodles, ingredients are here:

*FOR BOILING NOODLES:
1) 6 cup water.
2) 1 tsp salt.
3) 1 tsp oil.
4) 250 grams noodles.

FOR SCHEZWAN NOODLES:
1) 2 tsp olive oil
2) 3 clove garlic,(finely chopped).
3) 4 tsp spring onion, (chopped).
4) 1/2 onion, (finely chopped).
5) 4 tbsp cabbage.
6) 1/2 capsicum, (chopped).
7) 2 tbsp schezwan sauce.
8) 1/2 tsp salt.
